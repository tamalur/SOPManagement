Signing Off & Publishing SOP's to System

1.      SOP owners, approvers and reviewers must sign-off the SOP prior to publishing. The signatories can sign-off by clicking on their name as shown below:

 

 

2.      The following page will open and the signatories must check off on the box beside their name and the box at the bottom of the page to acknowledge that they have reviewed and approved the SOP. Click Submit to complete the sign-off.

 

 

3.      Once the owner, approver and reviewers have signed off on the SOP, the field for the signatories will no longer be yellow and Click to Publish will appear under the Publish column:

 

4.      Once the SOP is published, the below message will appear:

 

 

5.      The published SOP can be viewed by going to the SOP Library and clicking on the SOP Name. Note that the color under review date is green which means that the SOP active and not up for review.
