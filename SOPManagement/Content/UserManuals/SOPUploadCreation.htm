SOP Upload/Creation

1.      SOPs are created using Radiant’s SOP Management tool.

2.      From the SOP Dashboard, click on the “Create New SOP” button.

3.      The “SOP Create/Upload” screen will appear as follows:

 

 

 

4.      Click “choose file” to upload a pre-existing file (created in the in the SOP template) or enter the name of the SOP the “New SOP Name”:

 

5.      Select the folder of the department that the SOP belongs to.

a.      Select the subfolder of the department, if applicable.

 

6.      The SOP number will automatically populate to the next sequential number of the department (e.g. QAF-02). The SOP number cannot be changed or modified as it is system driven.

 

7.      Select the frequency of the SOP review (i.e. yearly, monthly, weekly).

 

8.      Select the “SOP Owner” by clicking on the drop-down menu.

 

9.      Select the “SOP approver” by clicking on the drop-down menu.

 

10.  Add reviewers by clicking on the drop-down menu and selecting “add”. Multiple reviewers can be added if required.

 

 

11.  Read-only access to the SOP can be by granted either of the following ways:

a.      Access to all employees: Click on “Read Access to All”

 

b.      Access only to departmental employees: Select the department from the drop-down menu.

 

c.       Access to individual employees: From “Add Viewers”, select employee(s) from the drop-down menu and click “Add”

 

 

 

 

12.  Click “Submit Form” to create the SOP.

 

13.  A message will appear to notify the user that the SOP has been successfully created. Click on the “SOP Dashboard” to complete the SOP.

 

 

14.  Click on the department to open SOP library. The folder will be yellow for the department:

 

15.  Click on the SOP name to launch the SOP from the SOP Library:

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

16.  MS Word will open under tracked changes. Click on “Open in Desktop App” to work on the SOP. Refer to the reference section of the Home Page of the SOP Management Tool on details of what to include in the sections of a SOP.

 

 

17.  Once the SOP is completed and reviewed. Follow the section from the Home Page on instructions to Sign-off and Publish the SOP.
